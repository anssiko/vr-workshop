Tentative Schedule

The workshop will focus around several topics identified by the expressions of interest. Each topic will be introduced by one or more related lightning talks, and will be explored more in-depth by discussion breakouts, concluded with joint summaries of the breakouts. The goal of each discussion is not to resolve the technical issues of the topic, but to determine its relevance and priority to standardization.

This schedule may change based on discussions with the program committee.
